Have you already our card? - the girl asked behind the counter.

Of course I have - I replied, taking out an overfull wallet.

I started looking for it, spreading dozens of other cards over the counter, just to find out that, last time I cleaned the wallet, I decided to left that card at home.

No problem! - the girl said - I'll make another one.

I start sweating, looking at all the cards in front of me, imagining how it will be to put them back in the wallet, together with the new one she is offering me. I just can't stand anymore cards. I need some rest.

Tired of carring dozens of cards, one for each of the brands you like? Tired of finding that, allways you need that special card, you just left it at home, because it didn't fit anymore in your wallet?

We have THE solution for you: THE ONE card.

It is the only card you need from now on. When you apply for it, we will merge your existing cards into it. When you go to your favorite shops, you just hand THE ONE card, and it is the same as handing the old shop card. And, if someone wants to give you another card, just tell him to put it into THE ONE.

FAQ

How much do you plan to charge the users?
THE ONE card hould be free: no setup costs, neither any type of annual fees. We think it is important to get crytical mass fast and, if we charge users, it will slow the adoption rate.
How do you plan to make money?
We think there will be several ways to make money:
  • The brands should pay to put their card in our system. If, as we believe, having just one card makes sense for the user, the brands will be pressed to join as the system gets adoption. Besides that, we would be able to offer new services to them. The loyalty card industry is big enougth to share some of its the revenues with us.
  • We will offer loyalty card services for small shops that can not afford to have their own.
  • As we build a loyal user base, we can make some money in adverting. We will own a shopping community, an interesting target to brands.
Do you plan to have credit or debit cards?
That is not our initial purpose. This does not mean that we can't evolve that way, if we realise it makes sense for the business. We are, in fact, talking about cards.
